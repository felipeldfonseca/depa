# Vision Brief — “AI Departments Platform”

The core idea:
I want to build a **web platform** that empowers micro-entrepreneurs and aspiring founders to launch and manage a business from scratch using **virtual departments powered by AI agents**. Instead of hiring a team or juggling dozens of disconnected SaaS tools, the user can activate a “Department” (like Marketing, Customer Service, Design, Finance, etc.), which comes pre-loaded with specialized AI agents that collaborate to perform tasks in that area.

The mission:
Make entrepreneurship radically more **accessible, fast, and affordable** by giving people a “company in a box” powered by AI. Even someone without an idea yet can start experimenting and testing businesses within minutes.

---

## Target Users

* **Micro-entrepreneurs** who have recently started or plan to start a small business.
* **Aspiring founders** who don’t even have an idea yet but want to try.
* **Side hustlers / freelancers** who want to scale operations without hiring staff.

The product should feel **futuristic but pragmatic**: sleek, modern, inspiring confidence, but also simple enough to get results fast.

---

## Core Concept

* The platform is **multi-tenant SaaS**, web-first (responsive, mobile-friendly).
* Users sign up, create their “company profile,” and then **activate Departments**.
* Each Department contains **multiple specialized AI agents** that collaborate.

  * Example: Marketing Department includes a Social Media agent, an SEO/Blog agent, an Ads agent, an Email agent.
  * Customer Service Department includes a WhatsApp Responder, FAQ Builder, Ticket Classifier, Escalation Manager.
* Departments can be configured via **templates and wizards** (industry presets like Restaurants, Clothing, Coaching, etc.).
* Departments produce real, actionable outputs: posts scheduled to Instagram, replies on WhatsApp, dashboards, financial summaries, etc.

---

## MVP Scope (Phase 1)

Focus on **two departments**:

1. **Marketing**: generate and schedule social posts, suggest ad creatives, basic blog/SEO support.
2. **Customer Service (CX)**: respond to WhatsApp messages, auto-classify inquiries, FAQ automation, basic escalation to human.

MVP must include:

* **Onboarding wizard** (“Create your company in minutes”)
* **Company dashboard**: see departments, KPIs, tasks.
* **Marketing tools**: create a calendar of posts, generate text+image, integrate with IG/FB.
* **CX tools**: connect WhatsApp, auto-reply with FAQ, dashboard of conversations.
* **Billing/Plans**: at least one paid plan (Stripe/Mercado Pago).
* **Templates**: starter packs for 2–3 industries.

---

## Long-Term Expansion (Phase 2+)

* Add more departments: Design, Data/BI, Sales CRM, Finance, Growth/Ads.
* Add **Marketplace**: allow third parties to create department packs or templates.
* Add **mobile app** once web is stable and adoption grows.
* Add **Academy**: micro-lessons and guides to empower entrepreneurs.

---

## Design & UX Principles

* **Neo-futuristic design**: dark mode first, bold typography, rounded edges, grid-based layout.
* **Clarity over complexity**: users should succeed in 1–2 clicks, no steep learning curve.
* **Gamified onboarding**: “Your company is live!”, progress bars, aha moments.
* **Trust & credibility**: safe, professional look; not gimmicky.

---

## Technical Principles

* **Stack**: Next.js (React, Tailwind) frontend; FastAPI backend; Postgres + Redis; GCP infra.
* **Orchestration**: AI agents coordinated via LangGraph/crewAI or custom orchestrator.
* **Multi-tenant**: isolation per company/org.
* **Observability**: SLOs for response time (<8s for content gen, <3s for replies).
* **Compliance**: LGPD-first (Brazil), GDPR-ready.
* **Security**: RBAC, MFA, data minimization, safe prompts, moderation guardrails.

---

## Why This Matters

Most people fail to start businesses because:

1. They don’t know where to begin.
2. They feel overwhelmed by the number of tools.
3. They lack resources to hire a team.

This platform eliminates those blockers:

* **No confusion** → Departments are pre-configured.
* **No tool overload** → Everything in one place.
* **No high costs** → Virtual team of AI agents at subscription price.

In short: we’re building the **first AI-powered company-builder**, where anyone can create a functional business in minutes.
